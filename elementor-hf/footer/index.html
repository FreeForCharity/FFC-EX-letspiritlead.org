  • OUR MISSION

Welcome to Let Spirit Lead, Inc.

Let Spirit Lead is a 501c3 nonprofit organization on a mission to ensure that every American has access to safe, decent, affordable housing in a thriving, resilient, and sustainable community. We are revolutionizing the community development and affordable housing space using innovation, coaching, and consulting.

10,000 hours volunteered
Learn More

Over 114,000 families in our region don’t have access to stable, affordable housing. At Let Spirit Lead, we equip faith-based organizations, entrepreneurs, and emerging leaders with the tools, training, and support to transform underutilized land, bold ideas, and deep-rooted passion into vibrant homes and thriving communities.

Whether you’re a church ready to develop housing, an aspiring affordable housing developer seeking your first opportunity, or a changemaker in need of community—our ecosystem of Spirit-led programs is here to help you build.

We combine innovation, coaching, and consulting to make sustainable impact possible—no matter your starting point.

You don’t need to have all the answers—you just need the calling.

We’ll walk with you from vision to reality.

Let us show you how to build for today, hope for tomorrow, and transform lives for generations.

If you feel called to be part of the solution, we’d love to connect!

  • Our solution

Four Integrated Programs, One Spirit-Led Mission

At Let Spirit Lead, we believe lasting transformation comes from equipping land stewards, community leaders, and faith-rooted organizations with the tools to build both housing and hope. That’s why our solution is not one-size-fits-all: It’s a Spirit-led ecosystem of four interconnected programs that meet people where they are:
Learn More

Yes In God’s Backyard (YIGBY) Coaching and Consulting Program

We guide churches and faith-based organizations through the early stages of developing affordable housing on underutilized land—with consulting, coaching, financing connections, and ongoing support.

Community Development Prep (CD Prep) Fellowship Program

Our workforce development initiative trains and equips emerging leaders (mostly from marginalized communities) for careers and entrepreneurship in real estate, affordable housing, and community development.

FutureForward Housing Development Initiative

Our goal is to directly build and own small-scale, eco-friendly, and community-centered manufactured housing communities—providing an innovative, replicable and scalable model for dignified homeownership and neighborhood resilience.

Let Spirit Lead
Grant Program

We offer quarterly microgrants to Spirit-led entrepreneurs, nonprofits, and small businesses advancing affordable housing and community development in communities across the country.

Together, these four programs provide practical support, purpose-driven pathways, and the prophetic imagination needed to create thriving, vibrant communities rooted in equity, dignity, and sustainable growth.

  • Who we are

Who we are

Let Spirit Lead is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to mobilizing the community to ensure everyone has access to safe, affordable housing within thriving, resilient, and sustainable neighborhoods.

Felicia Brown, an experienced real estate professional, founded Let Spirit Lead after discovering that over 114,000 families in the Inland SoCal region lack access to affordable housing. As a community development practitioner with a breadth and depth of experiences, Felicia understands the challenges you face. Yet, she firmly believes that there are no limits to what we can accomplish together.

Felicia’s passion, expertise, and faith drive our mission to equip community stakeholders with the tools and partnerships needed to turn underutilized land into places of hope and transformation.

  • OUR WHY

Why We Do What We Do

We believe Spirit-led people and organizations—especially those rooted in faith and community—are essential to solving the housing crisis and building lasting equity. Our calling is to equip them with the tools, training, and trust they need to turn vision into impact.

Thriving, sustainable communities begin with safe, stable housing—a basic need and human right for every individual. Why does this matter to you? Because the effects of the housing crisis impact us all, whether through our hearts or our pocketbooks.

How We Do What We Do

Our Process: listening first, guiding always. It all begins with listening to you —understanding your dreams, goals, and concerns. From there, we offer structured programming, tailored consulting, trusted connections, and valuable resources to help you answer the call to help solve the affordable housing crisis. With decades of experience in Southern California and a network of trusted partners, we’re here to turn your big dreams into tangible results.

Proud to be a trusted partner to EVERY client!

What You Can Do To Help

The housing crisis is complex, but every community member has the potential to be part of the solution. Here are three ways you can get involved:

Refer a FBO, Fellow, Funder, or Investor

Do you know someone passionate about affordable housing with a heart for serving their community? Introduce them to our mission! Your referral could be the first step in creating more affordable housing for families in need.

Donate

Your tax-deductible financial support helps us provide the resources, guidance, and partnerships needed to turn ideas into reality. Every dollar brings us closer to creating safe, affordable housing for those who need it most.

Pray

Join us in prayer for the housing crisis, for the community stakeholders considering this mission, and for the families who will one day call these homes their own. Pray for wisdom, guidance, and unity as we work together to make a difference.

Together, we can bring hope and healing to our community through the power of faith and action. Let’s manifest more places of refuge, stability, and renewal. Contact us today to learn more about how you can be part of the solution.
